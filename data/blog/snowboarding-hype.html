January 19, 2025

New snowboarding hype

After a longer break from snowboarding, I finally got back on the slopes for two weeks this January. I honestly forgot how much I love snowboarding and in general being on the mountain.

The feeling of carving down fresh powder, the crisp mountain air, and the incredible views reminded me why I fell in love with this sport in the first place. There's something magical about being up there, away from the daily grind, just focusing on the next turn.

This trip really reignited my passion for snowboarding. I'm already planning my next mountain adventure and looking into improving my technique. The mountains are calling, and I must go!

Here are some highlights from the trip:

  • Perfect powder conditions on day 3
  • Successfully attempted some intermediate slopes
  • Amazing sunrise views from the summit
  • Met some fellow snowboarding enthusiasts
  • Improved my carving technique significantly

Can't wait for the next adventure on the slopes!
