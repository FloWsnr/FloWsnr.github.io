My Hobbies

Snowboarding

Every winter, I escape to the mountains to enjoy the thrill of snowboarding. Whether it's carving down perfectly groomed slopes or exploring off-piste terrain, snowboarding offers me the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. I particularly enjoy riding in the Austrian and French Alps, where the combination of challenging terrain and breathtaking scenery creates an unforgettable experience.

Rock Climbing

Rock climbing challenges both my body and mind, requiring focus, strategy, and physical strength. I enjoy both indoor climbing at local gyms and outdoor climbing in natural settings. Each route presents a unique puzzle to solve, and the feeling of reaching the top after a challenging climb is incredibly rewarding.
